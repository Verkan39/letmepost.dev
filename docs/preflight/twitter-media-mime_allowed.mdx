---
title: twitter.media.mime_allowed
description: Twitter accepts jpeg/png/webp images, gif animations, and mp4 video.
---

## What it checks

For images: `mimeType ∈ { image/jpeg, image/png, image/webp, image/gif }`.
For videos: `mimeType === "video/mp4"`.

## Remediation

Re-encode unsupported formats.
