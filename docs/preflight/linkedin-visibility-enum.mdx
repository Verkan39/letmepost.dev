## What it checks

When the request body sets a LinkedIn-specific `visibility`, it must be one of `PUBLIC` or `CONNECTIONS`. Other values (LOGGED_IN, CONTAINER, etc.) require LinkedIn's MDP and aren't supported in v1.

## Remediation

Use `PUBLIC` (the default) or `CONNECTIONS`. The other visibility modes ship with org-posting in a follow-up slice.
